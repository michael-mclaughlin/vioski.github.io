ABOUT US

“I’m inspired by art, beauty and nature. Mid-century was my inspiration, but I wanted to bring it forward into the new-century. All my designs are totally comfortable, and inspired by the natural world. Each piece is customizable, and handcrafted to the highest quality standards.”

-Jeff Vioski, Founder and Designer

VIOSKI is new-century modern. Redefining modern furniture for the way we live and work today.

The influences of nature, art, simplicity, and architectural structure are integrated into our timeless, and thought-provoking furniture designs. Every piece is engineered for ultimate comfort and function. Extraordinary attention is devoted to every detail.

By design, VIOSKI furniture is a perfect fit for commercial installations. Our superior quality also meets the demands of commercial settings, as each furniture piece is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans in California.

From the day we launched VIOSKI in 2004, our relationships with our customers have been valued above all.

We pride ourselves on being true partners to our clients, and strive for perfection in every project. Our clients have expressed genuine gratitude for the way we’ve helped them deliver their ideal environments. It’s these partnerships that motivate us to be our very best.
